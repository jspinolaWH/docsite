# Pricing Model Overview

## Introduction

The WasteHero 2.0 pricing model represents a comprehensive system for managing product pricing, subscriptions, and billing across multiple markets.

## Core Components

### 1. Price Points
Price points define the actual cost of a product or service. They include:
- **Base price**: The fundamental cost before any modifications
- **Currency**: The denomination (EUR, USD, DKK, etc.)
- **Validity period**: Start and end dates for when the price is active

### 2. Price Modifiers
Modifiers allow dynamic pricing adjustments:
- **Volume discounts**: Reduced rates for higher quantities
- **Promotional pricing**: Temporary price reductions
- **Regional adjustments**: Market-specific pricing variations

### 3. Billing Cycles
Supported billing frequencies:
- Monthly
- Quarterly
- Annual
- One-time

## Product Categories

### Service Products
- Waste collection services
- Container rentals
- Special pickups

### Physical Products
- Containers
- Bins
- Accessories

## Integration Points

The pricing system integrates with:
- **CRM 2.0**: Customer and subscription management
- **Billing system**: Invoice generation
- **Analytics**: Pricing performance tracking
