PD-107 — Integration flow

The route for Kiertokapula: Customer bank (Nordea) → Ropo → WasteHero. Messages include invoice notifications, e-invoice orders, direct payment orders, and acceptance proposals. Changes arrive once per day.

PD-282 — Manual override protection

When the integration systematically returns the wrong e-invoice address (e.g. intermediary data error), the office can manually enter the correct address and mark it with a "do not overwrite" flag. Future integration updates skip this field.

PD-281 — Address as master data

WasteHero is the authoritative source. When a property management company changes in February, the new billing address updated in WasteHero is pushed immediately to the external invoicing system and applied to all open invoices and reminders — ensuring the new manager receives all correspondence.

Risk if not handled

Without address sync: payment reminders sent to old property manager who is no longer responsible. Customer disputes. Without e-invoice channel update: customer receives paper invoice despite having set up e-invoicing at their bank — generates customer service contacts and paper invoice surcharge disputes.
