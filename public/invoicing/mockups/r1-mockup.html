Invoice number sequence (PD-309)
Per PJH: first digit = 1 for public law, 2 for private law, 3 for credit notes. Second digit = year (6 = 2026). Last seven digits = running sequence. So 126000142 is the 142nd public-law invoice of 2026. Sequence numbers are never reused (accounts receivable requirement). Mixed-law customers get two parallel invoices, each with its own number.
Credit notes (hyvityslaskut)
PD-269 — credit invoices. Each credit note has its own sequential number (per PD-309), references the credited invoice, carries custom text and an internal comment, and produces a FINVOICE INV02 file. Full or partial credit supported.

Why credit notes are a first-class subtype
PD-269 specifies credit notes are standalone documents that carry their own custom text, internal comment, and an explicit reference to the credited invoice. FINVOICE distinguishes them with message code INV02 (separate from INV01). Both full and partial credit are supported — partial means crediting only selected invoice rows. Credited transactions remain visible in customer transaction history.

Release 1 — The Finnish foundation
Rebuild the underlying data model so it's correct for Finland. Ship one narrow end-to-end scenario on top of it that PJH can click through and validate. Five steps. Each step lives on one of the tabs above — click any step to jump to its screen.
01
Real event arrives
A bin emptying event from route execution lands in the new event schema with all PJH-required fields: VAT, cost centre, account, service responsibility, public/private law tag.

Event is priced and classified
Pricing engine applies VAT and tariff, then stamps the line with cost centre, accounting account, and public/private law tag — using the cost centres and accounts configured during PJH implementation.
→ Cost centres / Accounting accounts tabs
PD-296 PD-295 PD-284 PD-285
03
Invoice is generated
Events are bundled into one invoice. Invoice number comes from the Finnish-compliant sequence — 1xxxxxxxx for public law, 2xxxxxxxx for private law.
→ Invoices tab
PD-309
04
FINVOICE XML is produced
The invoice is serialised as a valid FINVOICE 3.0 INV01 XML message. Passes XSD validation and EN16931 business rules. Downloadable from the UI — not transmitted to Ropo in R1.
→ Invoice detail tab
PD-310
05
Credit note is issued
Office staff trigger a credit note against the invoice. It carries its own sequential number (3xxxxxxxx), references the original invoice, and is serialised as a FINVOICE INV02 message.
→ Credit notes tab
